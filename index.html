    ABOUT ICCAME-2025

    The International Conference on Contemporary Advances in Mechanical Engineering (ICCAME) has been held annually since 2019. The first conference was held in Chandigarh Engineering College-CGC, Landran, Mohali, Punjab – 140307 (INDIA), in July 2019. The second conference was held virtually in July 2020 due to the COVID-19 pandemic. The third and fourth conferences were held in August 2021 and September 2022. The upcoming International Conference on Contemporary Advances in Mechanical Engineering (ICCAME-2025) is scheduled to be held at Chandigarh Engineering College-CGC, Landran, Mohali, Punjab – 140307 (INDIA) . The conference has grown popularity over the years, attracting researchers and professionals from around the world to present their latest research findings and exchange ideas on the latest advances in mechanical engineering with a focus on physics and materials.

    AIM and SCOPE

    The primary goal of the International Conference on Contemporary Advances in Mechanical Engineering (ICCAME-2025), is to facilitate the exploration and discussion of the most current research trends in a wide range of fields, including Material Science and Engineering, application of physics principles in mechanical engineering, Design, Mechatronics, Control Systems , Robotics and Automation, Manufacturing processes and technologies, Industrial Engineering, Thermal & Fluids, Energy and Process Engineering, Automobile Engineering, as well as various interdisciplinary and emerging areas.

    ICCAME-2025 offers a unique opportunity for both national and international experts, as well as industry leaders, to collaborate and exchange their research experiences and future research aspirations. The conference will feature keynote speeches from prominent researchers in the field of mechanical engineering, physics, and astronomy. It will also include oral, poster presentations by researchers from around the world, showcasing their latest research findings and innovative ideas.

    Consequently, the conference serves as a dynamic platform for the promotion of swift communication and the exchange of scientific knowledge among scientists, researchers, academicians, industrialists, engineers, and students. Furthermore, ICCAME-2024 offers a platform for the publication of original research articles, review articles, and feature articles, thereby ensuring a diverse range of research outputs. We are proud to announce that all accepted papers will receive publication in SCOPUS indexed journals, enhancing the visibility and reach of the research presented at the conference.



    Join us at ICCAME-2025 to be a part of this collaborative effort to advance the fields of Mechanical Engineering and related disciplines through innovation, knowledge exchange, and collective endeavors.
